CSR Partnerships

Partner with ACT Foundation to drive meaningful social impact and fulfill your corporate social responsibility goals.

Why Partner With Us?

ACT Foundation offers a professional, transparent, and impact-driven platform for corporates to implement their CSR mandates.

Strategic Alignment

We align our projects with your company's values and CSR policy, ensuring strict adherence to Schedule VII of the Companies Act.

Compliance & Transparency

We maintain impeccable financial records and provide timely utilisation certificates and comprehensive impact reports.

Employee Engagement

We offer volunteering opportunities for your employees to directly engage with the community and experience the impact.

Pan-India Presence

Our network allows us to implement projects in various geographies, tailored to local needs.

Models of Engagement

  • Direct Implementation: We implement projects directly on your behalf.
  • Grant Management: We manage grants and monitor smaller grassroots NGOs.
  • Employee Volunteering: One-day or long-term volunteering programs.
  • Payroll Giving: Facilitating employee contributions for a cause.
