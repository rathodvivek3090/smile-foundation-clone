Statutory Compliance & Credibility

We pride ourselves on our transparency, accountability, and strict adherence to legal norms.

Registration & Legal Status

ACT Foundation is a duly registered non-profit organisation in India, established with the objective of undertaking charitable activities in the areas of education, healthcare, women empowerment, and environmental sustainability. The organisation operates in compliance with applicable laws, regulations, and governance standards prescribed by Indian authorities.

Section 12A Registration (Income Tax Act, 1961)

ACT Foundation has applied for registration under Section 12A of the Income Tax Act, 1961, and the application is currently under process with the concerned authorities.

Upon approval, the registration will formally recognise ACT Foundation as a charitable organisation for income tax purposes. In the interim, the Foundation continues to operate strictly on a not-for-profit basis, ensuring that all income and resources are utilised solely towards its stated charitable objectives.

Section 80G Certification (Income Tax Act, 1961)

ACT Foundation has applied for certification under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961, and the application is presently under review by the Income Tax Department.

Once approved, donors will be eligible to claim tax deductions on contributions made to the Foundation, as applicable under prevailing income tax laws. The organisation remains committed to full statutory compliance and transparent utilisation of donations during this period.

CSR Eligibility (Companies Act, 2013)

ACT Foundation has initiated the process to be recognised as an eligible CSR implementation agency in accordance with the Companies Act, 2013 and applicable CSR Rules.

While the registration and formal listings are under process, the Foundation actively engages in social initiatives aligned with Schedule VII activities and follows governance, documentation, and reporting practices expected under CSR frameworks.

ACT Foundation is open to working with corporate partners through compliant collaboration models, subject to mutual due diligence and statutory requirements.

Governance & Accountability

ACT Foundation follows strong governance practices to maintain donor and partner trust:

  • Clear separation of governance and execution roles
  • Regular financial oversight and internal controls
  • Transparent utilisation of funds
  • Documentation and reporting aligned with regulatory norms

Financial Transparency

All funds received by ACT Foundation are utilised strictly for programmatic and operational purposes in line with its charitable objectives. Financial records are maintained systematically and are available for review by authorised stakeholders as required by law.

Donor & Partner Assurance

ACT Foundation is committed to ethical fundraising, responsible spending, and honest communication. Donors and partners receive timely updates on program progress, fund utilisation, and impact outcomes, reinforcing trust and long-term collaboration.
