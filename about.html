About Us

ACT Foundation is an India-based non-profit organisation committed to inclusive development and social equity. We work at the grassroots to address systemic challenges affecting education, health, gender equality, and environmental sustainability.

Why We Exist

Despite progress, millions in India continue to face barriers to basic education, healthcare, safety, and environmental well-being. These challenges disproportionately affect marginalised and vulnerable communities.

ACT Foundation exists to bridge gaps through community-driven initiatives that are ethical, accountable, and sustainable.

Vision, Mission & Values

How We Work

Our methodology ensures that every intervention is sustainable, scalable, and community-led.

  1. Community needs assessment: Understanding the ground reality before intervention.
  2. Program design with local participation: Co-creating solutions for ownership.
  3. Resource mobilisation: Engaging donations, CSR, and partnerships.
  4. Transparent implementation: Executing with integrity and efficiency.
  5. Impact monitoring and reporting: Measuring outcomes and ensuring accountability.

ACT Foundation maintains compliance with applicable Indian laws and ethical standards, ensuring donor trust and accountability.
