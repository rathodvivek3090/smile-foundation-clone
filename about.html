About ACT Foundation

Committed to inclusive development, social equity, and community-led change.

Who We Are

ACT Foundation is an India-based non-profit organisation committed to inclusive development and social equity. We work at the grassroots to address systemic challenges affecting education, health, gender equality, and environmental sustainability.

Why We Exist

Despite progress, millions in India continue to face barriers to basic education, healthcare, safety, and environmental well-being. These challenges disproportionately affect marginalised and vulnerable communities.

ACT Foundation exists to bridge gaps through community-driven initiatives that are ethical, accountable, and sustainable.

Vision, Mission & Values

Vision

An India where every individual has access to education, health, dignity, and a safe environment.

Mission

To mobilise collective action and partnerships to empower communities through sustainable social interventions.

Core Values

  • Community-first approach
  • Transparency and accountability
  • Equity and inclusion
  • Collaboration and shared responsibility
  • Sustainability and long-term impact

Programs & Initiatives

Education Programs

  • Community learning centres
  • School support and digital education access
  • Youth skill development and career awareness
  • Parent and community engagement

Health Programs

  • Preventive health camps and screenings
  • Nutrition, hygiene, and maternal health awareness
  • Mental health sensitisation

Women Empowerment Programs

  • Skill training and livelihood support
  • Health, legal, and financial literacy
  • Support for self-help groups and local leadership

Environmental Programs

  • Tree plantation and conservation drives
  • Waste segregation and cleanliness campaigns
  • Environmental education at community level

How We Work

  1. Community needs assessment
  2. Program design with local participation
  3. Resource mobilisation (donations, CSR, partnerships)
  4. Transparent implementation
  5. Impact monitoring and reporting

ACT Foundation maintains compliance with applicable Indian laws and ethical standards, ensuring donor trust and accountability.

Statutory Compliance & Credibility

Registration & Legal Status: ACT Foundation is a duly registered non-profit organisation in India, established with the objective of undertaking charitable activities in the areas of education, healthcare, women empowerment, and environmental sustainability.

Section 12A Registration: Applied for and under process. We operate strictly on a not-for-profit basis.

Section 80G Certification: Applied for and under process. Once approved, donors will be eligible for tax deductions.

CSR Eligibility: Initiated process to be recognised as an eligible CSR implementation agency. We align with Schedule VII activities.

Governance & Accountability

ACT Foundation follows strong governance practices to maintain donor and partner trust:

  • Clear separation of governance and execution roles
  • Regular financial oversight and internal controls
  • Transparent utilisation of funds
  • Documentation and reporting aligned with regulatory norms
